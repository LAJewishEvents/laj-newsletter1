A note from Andrew
This is a big moment. This is the first official edition of LA Jewish Events Weekly — a centralized pulse of Jewish life across Los Angeles. For years, everything has lived in scattered group chats, forwarded flyers, and word-of-mouth threads. High energy — but fragmented. This changes that.

Each week, this brief will highlight what’s actually worth showing up to, surface meaningful community updates, and strengthen the connective tissue across Jewish LA. We’re starting with Purim — let’s do this properly.
— Andrew
